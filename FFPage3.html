On June 14, 2005, In Your Honor was released. It was a double album in commemoration of the band's 10 year anniversary. On September 25, 2007, Echoes, Silence, Patience & Grace was released. The band brought back Gil Norton, the same producer that helped make their second album back in 1997.

In Your Honor

In 2005, the Foo Fighters released a two-disc album. The first disc featured straight-forward rock songs. The second disc included acoustic, softer songs. A stadium tour was embarked on by the band but an acoustic, more intimate tour happened as well.

Echoes, Silence, Patience & Grace

In 2007, the Foo Fighters returned to the studio. They enlisted Gil Norton, the producer of their second album, to produce this new album. Pat Smear, who had only been a touring member of the band since 1997, rejoined officially for this record.

Medicine At Midnight

In 2021, the Foo Fighters released their tenth album. A year after the album's release, drummer Taylor Hawkins died in Columbia during a world tour. The loss shocked not only the band but also the fans, reminding everyone how fragile life really is.

But Here We Are

In 2023, the Foo Fighters released a new album after the death of Taylor Hawkins. Not only for the fans, but for the band, the album was cathartic and therapeutic. Currently, the band is on a tour with newly added drummer Josh Freese.
